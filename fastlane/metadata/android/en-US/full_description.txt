WA Auto Delete is a free, open-source Android app that automatically 
removes messages from contacts you want to ignore, directly inside 
WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business.

Simply add a contact name in the app, open a WhatsApp chat, and the 
service silently long-presses and deletes every visible message from 
that contact on your behalf. Works in both group chats and private chats.

No root access required. All processing happens entirely on your device. 
No data is collected, transmitted, or stored anywhere outside your phone.
